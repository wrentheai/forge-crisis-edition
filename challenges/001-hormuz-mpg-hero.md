## The Challenge

Improve your car's MPG by at least 10% this week using only scrap. No new parts. No mechanic. Just you, your garage, and Forge.

## Rules

1. Take a photo of your current MPG (trip computer, gas receipt math, whatever)
2. Apply Forge tips -- ask it: `"how to boost MPG by 15% without buying anything"`
3. Drive normally for one week
4. Screenshot your new MPG
5. Post before/after in #challenge-1 with **#HormuzMPGHero**

## Quick Wins to Try

- Check tire pressure (most people are 5-10 PSI low)
- Remove junk from your trunk (every 100 lbs = ~1% MPG loss)
- Clean your air filter (shake it out, vacuum it, reinstall)
- Tape over roof rack gaps (duct tape + cardboard = aero mod)
- Drive 60 instead of 75 on the highway (saves 15-25%)
- Coast to red lights instead of braking hard
- Windows up, vents open above 40 mph
